Our Mission to Reduce Emissions

We are the Gallagher Intern Class at 181 University Ave in Toronto! We are a passionate group of individuals who understand the significance of air travel, especially within the insurance industry. However, we also recognize the alarming levels of carbon emissions associated with this mode of transportation, which directly contribute to the destruction of our environment.

To address this issue, we have developed a Carbon Calculator app. With just a few simple steps, you can determine the amount of CO2 you have emitted through air travel in the past year and the number of trees required to offset it.

For every tree equivalent, we receive in donations, we will plant the corresponding number of trees. Together, we can actively contribute to reforestation efforts and combat climate change.

Calculation: [ (Average Domestic Flight Length) * (Average kg of Carbon Emissions Emissions Per Domestic Flight km) ] / [Average emissions collected from a tree over its lifetime] = [(807.89)*(.255)] / [500]

The average domestic flight length in the US is 807.89 km (BTS, 2018) and the average emissions per km of domestic flight is 255g per km (Our World In Data, 2018). The average Carbon captured from a tree over a lifetime can vary but often averages out at 500kg over it's lifetime (Statista, 2020).
